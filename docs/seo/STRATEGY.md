# ToolsByTask — SEO/GEO 作战方案

_Generated from multi-agent recon. Goal metric: Google Search Console impressions growth. White-hat only._

## 0. 定位

**ToolsByTask is a decision engine for picking ONE AI tool to do ONE job** — not another 10,000-tool encyclopedia. It serves the high-intent buyer in the **shortlist-and-decide moment**: someone who has already named the job ("write SEO blogs," "take meeting notes," "generate product images") and is comparing 2–4 finalists on price, free tier, and fit *right before they click "Start free trial."* That is the highest-converting point in the funnel and exactly where affiliate commissions + Stripe Featured placements pay out.

**Who:** SMB owners/solopreneurs (budget + free-tier sensitive), freelancers/agencies (need commercial-use fit, distrust vendor blogs), marketers/content leads (team features, high-LTV), indie devs (head-to-head confirmation), and the highest-intent segment — **switchers** searching "[tool] alternatives / cheaper than [X]" with a wallet already open.

**The decision moment to own:** the click *after* TheresAnAIForThat — "okay, which of these is right for ME, and why." Incumbents optimize for discovery ("look how many tools exist!"); ToolsByTask optimizes for commitment. The wedge is **honesty + structure**: machine-comparable pricing/free-tier data + human `bestFor`/`notFor` editorial that no incumbent has at scale, kept current by the weekly ingester.

### 独有资产
- bestFor / notFor honest editorial one-liners on all 140 tools — the trust differentiator readers currently flee to Reddit to find; NO incumbent (TAAFT upvotes, Futurepedia popularity scores) has this at scale
- Structured pricingText + hasFreeTier boolean across all 140 tools — a machine-comparable price/free-tier layer the directories completely lack; this single dataset powers an entire class of pages (free-tier filters, pricing index, 'under $X' pages) competitors structurally cannot generate
- Task-first taxonomy: 13 job categories + useCases[] + tags[] — data shaped around jobs and constraints, so 'best X for [persona]' and 'best free X' pages are real filtered queries against real data, not prose listicles
- Weekly Python ingester + daily ISR — a freshness engine that keeps pricing/comparison data current; 2025 Medium listicles rot, and 2026 evidence shows content updated within 12 months earns ~3.2–4.3x more AI citations
- Programmatic generation capability over structured data — can mint comparison / alternatives / 'best X for [persona/budget/free-tier]' pages, each with genuine unique data (not thin shells) because each is backed by real bestFor/notFor + pricing
- Canonicalized /[a]-vs-[b] compare route already shipped with alphabetical dedup — a working comparison engine ready to extend to 3-way and standalone alternatives pages
- Affiliate-first Visit button (logged via /api/go) + Stripe Featured — monetization sits precisely at the decision moment, so editorial quality and revenue compound instead of fighting
- Greenfield + zero ranking history is paradoxically an asset for GEO: ~90% of what ChatGPT cites is NOT in Google's top 10, so the site can get LLM-cited NOW, before it ranks — a faster path to impressions than classic SEO

### 花钱/决策的关键时刻
- About to click 'Start free trial' on a tool after comparing 2–4 finalists — the affiliate Visit button is the conversion (highest commission moment)
- Switcher deciding to cancel [current tool] and move to a cheaper/better alternative — '[tool] alternatives' / 'cheaper than [X]' pages capture a buyer mid-switch
- Budget-sensitive buyer filtering on 'has a free tier' before committing a credit card — the hasFreeTier flag is the deciding factor right before signup
- Marketer/content-lead with approval authority choosing a team-tier tool ('best AI writer with brand voice for a team') — high-LTV affiliate conversion
- Comparing exact head-to-head ('Cursor vs Copilot', 'Synthesia vs HeyGen') to confirm a near-decided choice — the Visit button on the winning side of the compare table
- A tool vendor deciding to buy a Featured placement after seeing real outbound click volume on their category — the Stripe monetization moment
- Reading the 'Not for' verdict and deciding a tool is wrong for them — redirects to the alternative on the same page (saves the click for ToolsByTask's commission instead of a bounce)

### 能赢的缝隙

**The "decide between the finalists" layer for CONSTRAINED buyers — structured, filterable, honest, and current — which neither directories nor listicles can reach.** Concretely, own three SERP territories the incumbents structurally can't: (1) **constraint-qualified pages** — "best free AI [task]", "best AI [task] for [persona]", "best AI [task] under $X for commercial use" — backed by the hasFreeTier flag + useCases[] + pricingText, served as a filterable/comparable/dated table instead of prose; (2) the **switcher set** — "[tool] alternative free" / "[tool] alternatives" — the weakest-SERP, highest-intent cluster on the whole map (ranking domains today are brand-new/thin: maginary.ai, zeroskillai.com, diginewbie); (3) **"honest" as the brand identity** — "the directory that tells you what NOT to buy." Directories have the tools but no editorial/price structure; blogs have the angle but no live filterable data. ToolsByTask sits in the intersection neither side can cheaply retrofit. Win the click AFTER TheresAnAIForThat, not against it.

## 2. 选定的奇招(白帽)

### The Honest 'AI Tool Pricing & Free-Tier Index' — original-data linkable asset  _(effort: high / impact: high)_
- **为什么适合本站**: ToolsByTask is the ONLY site that can publish this: 140 tools x structured pricingText x hasFreeTier x 13 categories is a proprietary dataset nobody else has. Journalists and bloggers link to original DATA, not opinions; original-data pages are disproportionately AI-cited as stat sources ('X% of AI writing tools have a free tier', 'median starting price by category', 'cheapest tool that does Y'). The weekly ingester makes it repeatably refreshable = recurring PR + compounding backlinks, which is exactly what a greenfield domain with zero authority needs most.
- **怎么做**: Build a /data (or /ai-tools-pricing-report) aggregation page from the existing DB: % free-tier by category, median/min starting price by category, free-vs-paid distribution, count of tools under $X. Render real HTML tables + simple charts + a 40-60 word answer capsule per stat. Add a 'cite this data' block with a copy-paste citation + permalink. Refresh quarterly via the ingester; date-stamp it. Pitch the stats to AI/SaaS newsletters and tool bloggers for the backlink.

### Constraint-qualified pages: 'best free AI [task]' + gated 'best AI [task] for [persona]'  _(effort: medium / impact: high)_
- **为什么适合本站**: This is the largest programmatic surface AND the lowest-competition winnable SERP for a new site — 'best free AI X' is mid-strength listicles, 'best AI X for [persona]' is pure thin-affiliate with zero brand moat (confirmed live: aistudymaster, creatorsroot). The hasFreeTier flag + useCases[] + bestFor/notFor are literally the filter and persona signals these queries need, so each page is a genuine filtered table with honest editorial — NOT a thin doorway. Comparison/'best for' formats are also the #1 AI-cited shape.
- **怎么做**: Add /best/[task]/free (filter listings on hasFreeTier, add 'free tier limits' column) and /best/[task]/for/[persona] (match useCases[]/tags to a curated, autocomplete-validated persona list: students, YouTubers, Shopify sellers, small business, agencies, beginners, solo devs). HARD GUARDRAIL: only render a page if >=4 qualifying tools exist AND it has unique editorial verdicts — never ship an empty shell. Each page gets an answer capsule + a comparable table + FAQ schema.

### Switcher set: standalone '[tool] alternatives' + '[tool] alternative free' pages  _(effort: medium / impact: high)_
- **为什么适合本站**: The single weakest SERP cluster on the entire map (ranking domains are brand-new/thin) AND the highest purchase-intent — a switcher is a buyer with a wallet already open, the exact monetization moment. The alternatives section already exists inside /[slug]; promoting it to a standalone indexable page with notFor one-liners + a pricing/free-tier comparison table beats AlternativeTo on editorial and listicles on data. The 'free' variant filters on hasFreeTier — a native, auto-generated advantage.
- **怎么做**: Add /[slug]/alternatives (same-category set, excluding the tool, with a comparison table: pricing, free-tier, bestFor/notFor) and a free-filtered view for the 'alternative free' query. Add internal links from every /[slug] detail page and from /best pages. Bottom-line verdict capsule: 'The best free alternative to X is Y because…'. Canonicalize and add FAQPage schema ('Is there a free alternative to X?').

### 3-way comparison matrix (avoid leading with 2-way)  _(effort: medium / impact: high)_
- **为什么适合本站**: Vendors structurally CANNOT build 3-way comparisons (they won't feature two competitors), so the SERP is weak Medium/small-directory content — confirmed live (aitools-directory, alternates.ai). ToolsByTask already ships a working canonicalized 2-way /[a]-vs-[b] engine and has the structured comparable data (pricing/free/use-cases) to generate honest 3-way tables instantly. This sidesteps the 2-way trap where vendor pages own #1, and 3-way tables are a top AI-cited format.
- **怎么做**: Extend the versus slug logic to support 3 slugs (/compare/[a]-vs-[b]-vs-[c], canonicalized alphabetically). Generate only SENSIBLE trios (same category, top tools by featured rank) with a cap to avoid combinatorial spam. Real HTML comparison table + a 'Bottom line: pick A if…, B if…, C if…' verdict capsule above it + FAQPage schema. Pre-render the highest-demand trios via generateStaticParams.

### GEO foundation: answer capsules + FAQ/Review schema + visible dateModified + llms.txt + robots fix  _(effort: medium / impact: high)_
- **为什么适合本站**: Greenfield with zero ranking history means GEO is the FASTEST path to impressions — ~90% of ChatGPT citations are outside Google's top 10, so the site can get cited before it ranks. 72.4% of ChatGPT-cited pages have a 40-60 word answer capsule under an H2; freshness buys 3-4x citations. The weekly ingester + daily ISR is a structural freshness advantage almost no listicle has — but it's wasted unless surfaced. The current robots.ts uses a bare wildcard + a non-standard host: directive, risking accidental bot blocks; seo.ts has ZERO FAQ/Review schema and no dateModified.
- **怎么做**: 1) Rewrite robots.ts with explicit per-bot allow groups (GPTBot, OAI-SearchBot, ChatGPT-User, ClaudeBot, anthropic-ai, PerplexityBot, Google-Extended, Bingbot, Applebot-Extended), drop host:, and verify Vercel WAF isn't 403-ing those bots. 2) Add a 40-60 word answer capsule under the H1 on every /best, /compare, and /[slug] page. 3) Add faqPageLd() to seo.ts generating 3-5 Q&As from existing data (free plan? pricing? best for?) rendered as VISIBLE matching HTML. 4) Thread a visible + schema dateModified fed by the ingester run date, plus a 'Pricing verified [date]' line with an outbound source link. 5) Ship /llms.txt + /llms-full.txt rendered from the DB.

## 6. 人类待办(机器替不了)
